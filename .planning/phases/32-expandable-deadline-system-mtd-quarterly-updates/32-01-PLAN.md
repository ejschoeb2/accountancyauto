   - `mtd_quarterly_update` | 'MTD Quarterly Update' | 'HMRC Making Tax Digital quarterly submission' | calculator_type='mtd_quarterly' | applicable: {Sole Trader, Partnership} | sort_order=6
   - `confirmation_statement` | 'Confirmation Statement' | 'Companies House annual confirmation' | calculator_type='confirmation_statement' | applicable: {Limited Company, LLP} | sort_order=7
   - `p11d_filing` | 'P11D Filing' | 'Employee benefits and expenses return' | calculator_type='fixed_annual' | applicable: {Limited Company, LLP, Partnership} | sort_order=8
   - `paye_monthly` | 'PAYE Monthly' | 'Monthly PAYE payment to HMRC' | calculator_type='monthly_22nd' | applicable: {Limited Company, LLP, Partnership, Sole Trader} | sort_order=9
   - `cis_monthly_return` | 'CIS Monthly Return' | 'Construction Industry Scheme monthly return' | calculator_type='monthly_19th' | applicable: {Limited Company, LLP, Partnership, Sole Trader} | sort_order=10
   - `payroll_year_end` | 'Payroll Year-End' | 'Final payroll submission for the tax year' | calculator_type='fixed_annual' | applicable: {Limited Company, LLP, Partnership, Sole Trader} | sort_order=11
   - `sa_payment_on_account` | 'SA Payment on Account' | 'Self Assessment second payment on account' | calculator_type='fixed_annual' | applicable: {Sole Trader, Partnership, Individual} | sort_order=12
   - `partnership_tax_return` | 'Partnership Tax Return' | 'Annual partnership tax return filing' | calculator_type='fixed_annual' | applicable: {Partnership} | sort_order=13
   - `trust_tax_return` | 'Trust Tax Return' | 'Annual trust and estate tax return' | calculator_type='fixed_annual' | applicable: {Individual} | sort_order=14

4. Create `org_filing_type_selections` table:
   ```sql
   CREATE TABLE org_filing_type_selections (
     org_id UUID NOT NULL REFERENCES organisations(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
     filing_type_id TEXT NOT NULL REFERENCES filing_types(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,
     is_active BOOLEAN NOT NULL DEFAULT true,
     activated_at TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT now(),
     PRIMARY KEY (org_id, filing_type_id)
   );
   ```

5. Enable RLS on `org_filing_type_selections`:
   - SELECT policy for authenticated: `USING (org_id = auth_org_id())`
   - No INSERT/UPDATE/DELETE policies (service_role only)

6. Backfill existing orgs with the 5 default types:
   ```sql
   INSERT INTO org_filing_type_selections (org_id, filing_type_id, is_active)
   SELECT o.id, ft.id, true
   FROM organisations o
   CROSS JOIN filing_types ft
   WHERE ft.is_seeded_default = true
   ON CONFLICT (org_id, filing_type_id) DO NOTHING;
   ```

Use `ON CONFLICT DO NOTHING` for all INSERT statements for idempotency.
